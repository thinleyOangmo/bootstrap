Terms of reference

All the guests are requested to produce valid passport with minimum of six months validity, approved Bhutan visa copy at the time of check-in at the hotel. Indian Nationals are requested to produce either Voters ID / Passport with minimum validity of six months during their period of visit in Bhutan and valid travel permit at the time of check in at the hotel.


    The Package Rates are inclusive of the following services:
  • MAP packages are inclusive of room (twin sharing basis), breakfast & dinner.
  • CP packages are inclusive of room (twin sharing basis) and breakfast only.
  • All public areas with free Wi-Fi wherever available.

    • The management of BHR will be offering 01 bottled mineral water per room per day. The Package Rates are exclusive of the following services: Travel & Medical insurance coverage and medical treatment of the guests. Drinks/Beverages. Communication charges, laundry, tips, porters at the hotels. Personal expenses of any nature. Pick up and drop at the hotel from/to airport can be arranged on request and subject to confirmation of the payment.

    TERMS AND CONDITIONS


  • All reservations have to be sent in written format by e-mail. The bookings have to be sent or confirmed on or before 30 days prior to the date of check-in at the hotel. Non confirmation of the bookings without any notice after 30 days will be treated as cancelled and the reservations will be released automatically from the system to avoid cancellation charges.

    • Any bookings received/reinstated after 30 days period will be accepted depending upon the availability of the room & subject to confirmation of the full payment.


  • Packages/services once booked and confirmed will be subject to the cancellation policy. Please refer to CANCELLATIONS

  • The package rates are subject to specific facilities as agreed between the guests and the hotel management. Any extras thus incurred due to deviation/ alternation from the actual packages booked, after confirmation of the packages, will be chargeable to the guest and liable for payment prior to check-out time.

  • Every effort will be made to adhere to the planned reservation. However, due to unforeseen circumstances such as over-booking, non-receipt of the payment, weather and road conditions, changes in flight schedules, national holidays and reasons beyond the control of BHR, the staff and the management of BHR will not be responsible for any changes in the daily activities of the hotel and non-confirmation of the room.

  • All rates and conditions applied above are subject to change depending upon the policies of the Royal Government of Bhutan, which shall be intimated to you as soon as our office is notified officially about it.

  • There is no personal insurance of any kind included in the price. Travel insurance should be obtained from your place of residence, before commencement of your trip and prior to check in at the hotel. The management of BHR will not be held liable for any illness, injury or death sustained during your stay in the hotel.
